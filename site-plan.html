Home page

Hello, I'm Benjamin Petty, currently a freshman at Brigham Young University-Idaho, where I am a degree in computer science. My journey into the world of programming began at a young age, and over the years, I have acquired a substantial breadth of experience across a multitude of programming languages. My proficiency spans languages such as C++, Python, HTML, JavaScript, and SQL, reflecting a strong foundation in both high-level and web development languages. While my programming skills continue to evolve, I have unwavering enthusiasm for expanding my knowledge and technical capabilities. My educational pursuit in computer science is not merely an academic endeavor but a lifelong commitment to keeping pace with the ever-evolving tech landscape. I am excited about embracing emerging technologies, mastering new programming languages, and contributing to innovative solutions in the digital world. Beyond the realm of coding, I am equally passionate about the world of music. I find joy in creating music and sharing it with others. Whether it's playing a musical instrument or lending my voice through singing, I believe that music has the power to connect people on a profound level. It provides an escape from the stresses of life and offers a platform for self-expression and shared experiences. I also enjoy reading and possess a strong appreciation for books. Reading is a favorite pastime that allows me to explore different worlds, stimulate my imagination, and gain a deeper understanding of various perspectives. Moreover, I've always had a knack for organization. I invest a significant amount of my free time and energy in bringing order to chaos, be it in my personal space, digital files, or daily routines. This meticulous approach to organization not only enhances productivity but also reflects my commitment to efficiency and excellence in all aspects of life. As I embark on my academic and personal journey, my vision is to seamlessly integrate my technical prowess, musical passions, literary interests, and organizational skills. These elements collectively define my path forward, and I invite you to explore my website to learn more about the projects and experiences that are shaping my future. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing more of my journey with you.

About Me

I am the eighth child in a family of ten, and my the entirety of my childhood years were spent in North Carolina, where I received K-12 homeschooling. This early educational experience instilled in me values such as independence and self-discipline, which have had a lasting impact on my personal and professional development. My interest in technology and programming was piqued at an early age, around ten, when I embarked on a JavaScript course on Khan Academy. I vividly recall creating my first JavaScript game on Khan Academy at the age of 13, using the platform's built-in library. This marked the beginning of my journey into the world of coding. Over the years, I broadened my horizons by delving into Python and C++, as well as gaining proficiency in HTML, CSS, and SQL. My career aspirations in technology encompass several facets. While I am committed to honing my skills in lower-level languages like C and C++ to specialize as a back-end software developer, I am also deeply interested in game development and cybersecurity. These pursuits reflect my ambition to have a well-rounded and versatile career in the tech industry. In addition to my technological interests, I am passionate about music. I began learning the piano at the age of six and have enjoyed playing various musical instruments, as well as singing. My love for music has translated into performances at recitals, talent shows, and in the setting of my local church. Music is not just a pastime for me; it's an integral part of my identity, and I am dedicated to its continued presence in my life. Beyond my technical and musical pursuits, I have actively sought opportunities for personal and professional growth. This has included various part-time jobs, such as working at a grocery store and as a custodian. These experiences have strengthened my teamwork, communication, and leadership skills. In terms of leadership, I have served in various capacities within my local church, including holding class presidencies in my youth group. My involvement in the Boy Scouts of America program allowed me to take on leadership roles within my troop, culminating in achieving the rank of Life Scout. These experiences have not only shaped my character but also equipped me with essential leadership, organizational, and communication skills. Colleagues and acquaintances often describe me as affable and easy to work with. However, underlying my friendly demeanor is a highly analytical, logical, and organized mindset. I love to solve problems and learn to see concepts in different ways, but I also thrive in collaborative environments and value the opportunity to connect with and understand people on a deeper level. In summary, my life has been marked by a diverse range of experiences, interests, and aspirations. From coding to music, leadership roles to personal growth, my journey continues to unfold with abundant opportunities and a resolute commitment to excellence. Explore my personal website to learn more about my endeavors and connect with me as I progress through this exciting journey. Thank you for joining me on this path!
